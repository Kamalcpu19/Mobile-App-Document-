# Invoice Tracking

### Description

Users can monitor invoices with outstanding balances and follow up with customers for payment completion.

### Features

- View pending invoice list
- Track payment status per customer
- Monitor total pending payment counts

### Expected Result

Invoice and payment information should display accurately for billing staff follow-up.
