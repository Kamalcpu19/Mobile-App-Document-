# Quick Reply & Actions

### Available Actions

- **Quick Reply** — send predefined or custom responses
- **Call Option** — initiate a call to the customer
- **Camera Attachment** — attach photos from camera
- **Create RO Access** — start a new repair order from conversation
- **Profile Access** — open profile settings

### Expected Result

Users should be able to perform quick actions without leaving the conversation screen.
