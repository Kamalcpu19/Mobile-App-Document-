# Customer Conversations

### Description

Users can view and manage ongoing customer conversations related to service requests, follow-ups, and workshop communications.

### Communication Flow

1. Customer Messages
2. View Customer Conversations
3. AI Complaint Detection
4. Quick Reply / Call / Camera Actions

### Expected Result

Users should be able to respond to customer messages efficiently and track conversation history.
